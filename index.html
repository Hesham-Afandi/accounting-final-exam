🔹 Part 3: Case Study Analysis (15 Marks)

Case: GreenTech Solutions
GreenTech manufactures solar panels. They need to: evaluate new market profitability, provide audited statements to investors, undergo a tax review, implement real-time inventory software, and publish an ESG impact report.
  1. Which branch for new market profitability? Why?
    ✅ Managerial/Cost – analyzes costs, revenues & break-even for decisions.
  2. Statements for investors? Which branch ensures reliability?
    ✅ Financial Statements; Auditing ensures reliability.
  3. Branch for tax review? TWO documents?
    ✅ Tax Accounting; Tax Returns, Compliance/Provision Reports.
  4. System/branch for real-time tracking? ONE benefit?
    ✅ AIS; real-time data, error reduction, automation.
  5. Branch for environmental report? TWO metrics?
    ✅ Sustainability; CO₂ reduced, % renewable/recycled materials.
